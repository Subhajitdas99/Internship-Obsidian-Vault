## üí° Tips for Effective Mentorship

### Make the Most of Your Mentorship

1. **Be Proactive**
   - Don't wait for mentors to check on you
   - Share your progress regularly
   - Ask for feedback actively

2. **Be Specific**
   - Frame clear questions
   - Provide context for your problems
   - Share error messages and logs

3. **Be Receptive**
   - Accept constructive criticism
   - Implement feedback promptly
   - Show appreciation for guidance

4. **Be Professional**
   - Maintain formal communication
   - Meet deadlines consistently
   - Follow up on commitments
